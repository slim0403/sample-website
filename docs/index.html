Our Services

Business Cases

SPA can assist you to make an informed infrastructure investment decision, underpinned by a robust business case, including:

  • Bringing clarity to the need case/business issue
  • Identifying options to address the need case
  • Developing robust, evidence-based cost benefit analysis of the options to determine the recommended option
  • Crafting well justified business case narrative to inform decision making and facilitate Executive/Board approval
Regulatory Submissions to the Australian Energy Regulator (AER)

SPA can assist you with preparing well justified regulatory submissions and documents based on a deep understanding and extensive experiences with the Australian electricity regulatory framework. This includes:

  • Revenue proposals that demonstrate prudent and efficient investments, leveraging experiences with electricity transmission and distribution revenue proposals
  • Regulatory investment tests (RITs)
  • Regulatory reporting (i.e. regulatory information order)
Asset Management

SPA can assist you with developing or reviewing asset management strategies and plans that consider the asset whole lifecycle, including:

  • Identifying asset issues/pain points based on data analysis
  • Developing options to address these asset issues
  • Developing robust, evidence-based cost benefit analysis of the options to determine the most efficient whole of asset lifecycle option
  • Developing an asset management plan that aligns with the asset management strategy
Strategy for Infrastructure Assets

SPA can assist you with developing infrastructure strategy and plans to achieve your business objectives, including:

  • Articulating current state
  • Envisioning future state
  • Gap analysis of current state and future state
  • Developing roadmap from current state to future state
Project Management

SPA can assist you with managing your challenging project that requires close management of multiple stakeholders and deadlines with competing priorities and non-negotiable deadline such as the revenue proposals to the AER.
