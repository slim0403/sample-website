Why SPA

What we do

Five ways we help you invest with confidence.

Business cases

A robust, evidence-based case for the investment decision in front of you. We:

  • Bring clarity to the need case and the business issue behind it
  • Identify the options that genuinely address it
  • Build evidence-based cost–benefit analysis to find the recommended option
  • Craft the narrative that earns executive and board approval

Regulatory submissions to the AER

Well-justified submissions grounded in the Australian electricity regulatory framework, including:

  • Revenue proposals that demonstrate prudent, efficient investment across transmission and distribution
  • Regulatory investment tests (RITs)
  • Regulatory reporting and information orders

Asset management

Strategies and plans that consider the whole asset lifecycle. We:

  • Identify asset issues and pain points through data analysis
  • Develop the options that address them
  • Compare options on whole-of-lifecycle cost and benefit
  • Produce a plan aligned with your asset management strategy

Infrastructure strategy

A clear path from where your assets are to where your business needs them. We:

  • Articulate the current state honestly
  • Set out a future state worth reaching
  • Run the gap analysis between the two
  • Build the roadmap to close it

Project management

Hands-on management of the projects where stakeholders are many, priorities compete, and the deadline doesn't move — such as a revenue proposal to the AER.
